About Puja Enterprise – Saree, Kurti & Salwar Manufacturer in West Bengal

Puja Enterprise is a trusted garment manufacturer in Hooghly, West Bengal, specializing in the production of high-quality sarees, kurtis, and salwar sets. We focus on superior fabric selection, precise stitching, and modern designs to meet the growing demand of retailers and wholesalers across India. Our commitment to quality, competitive pricing, and timely delivery makes us a reliable partner in the ethnic wear manufacturing industry.
